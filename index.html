Nestor's Lechon

Professional catering services specializing in traditional Filipino roasted pig

Nestor's Lechon specializes in traditional Filipino lechon preparation using time-honored cooking methods. Our lechon is slow-roasted over charcoal for optimal flavor and texture, featuring crispy skin and tender, juicy meat.

We cater to weddings, corporate events, celebrations, and private gatherings throughout Bacoor City, Cavite and surrounding areas. Each order is prepared with careful attention to quality and food safety standards.
